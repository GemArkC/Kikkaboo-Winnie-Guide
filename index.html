Hands-Free Comfort, Powerful Performance, Anytime, Anywhere

The WINNIE wearable electric breast pump offers a hands-free, portable solution for modern moms, combining comfort, convenience, and advanced features. With multiple modes and adjustable suction, this pump makes expressing milk easy and efficient, whether at home or on the go.

Four Pumping Modes - Choose from Stimulation, Massage, Pumping, and Bionic mode, with 9-level suction force adjustment to suit your comfort and needs.

Hands-Free and Portable - The innovative design is completely wearable and cordless, allowing you to pump discreetly and comfortably anywhere.

User-Friendly Features - Includes a memory function, anti-backflow protection, and a lightweight, quiet operation with easy-to-clean parts for added convenience.

Long-Lasting Battery - Powered by a large 1500 mAh lithium battery with a 140ml Tritan plastic milk container, offering approximately 2 hours of working time and 3 hours of charging time. 

Experience true mobility with Winnie’s hands-free design, eliminating the hassle of external cords and tubes. This wearable pump allows you to express milk discreetly and efficiently no matter where you are.

Ensure optimal comfort and efficiency with three different sizes of silicone breast shields (18mm, 20mm, and 22mm) included, allowing you to find the perfect fit for your body and enhance the pumping experience.

Choose from four specialized modes: Stimulation, Massage, Pumping, and Bionic, each with 9 levels of suction force adjustment to cater to your specific comfort and efficiency needs throughout your breastfeeding journey.

The milk container is crafted from durable Tritan plastic, ensuring it’s safe and free from harmful chemicals. With a capacity of 140ml, it offers ample storage for expressed milk, ready for feeding time or storage.
